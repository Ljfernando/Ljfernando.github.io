About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Lance and I am a student at the University of San Francisco studying in the Data Science program. As an aspiring Data Scientist I enjoy diving head first into datasets and exploring the treasures within. I can spend hours playing around with data from Kaggle or other sources and reading about cool ML algorithms or new innovations in the field. Aside from data-related ventures, I also take pleasure in music. I grew up learning how to play different instruments and singing in different genres of music from Dixieland Jazz to Musical Theater. I am also an avid cyclist which helps to keep a healthy lifestyle and to feed my hunger for adrenaline.
